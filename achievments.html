Mandelbrot is best known for is his research on fractals, discovering the Mandelbrot Set, and his theory of roughness. He explained that objects are never smooth and so there must be a mathematical formula to express how rough an object is.

We will go more in depth on the Mandelbrot set on the facts page . For now, just know that it is a graph of the iterative function z = z^2 + c on the complex plane that has literally infinite depth of detail, or roughness.
